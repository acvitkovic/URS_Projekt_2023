Igre se biraju korištenjem tipki mikrokontrolera. Prva igra sa tipkom 1(u kodu 0), druga igra sa tipkom 2(u kodu 1) i rezultat te novi početak sa tipkom 3(u kodu 2).
Prva igra je implementirana koristeći MyoWare Muscle Sensor spojen na ATmega32A mikrokontroler putem ADC kanala 0.

Games are choosen by implementing microcontroller buttons. First game is choosen nwith Key1(0 in code), second game with Key2(1 in code), and score and reset with Key3(2 in code).
First game is implemented using MyoWare muscle sensor connected to ATmega32A onn ADC channel 0.
